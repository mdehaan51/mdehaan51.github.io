About Mike DeHaan

X

Hi, I'm Mike!

My path to development is a bit atypical. Growing up in Bozeman Montana I was never around much tech. I didn't grow up coding, nor did I know anyone that did. Growing up, having a strong interest in computers made me outlier amongst the other local kids who tended to not have much interest in tech.

Upon graduating from High School, I went to Gonzaga University in Spokane, WA and got an engineering degree. I wasn't necessarily interested in engineerig but what else are you supposed to do when your strengths have always been Math and Science? Long story short, after getting that degree and spending 5 years unhappily working as an engineer, I decided I needed to pursue something else.

I left my engineering job and started teaching myself coding from scratch. It was a daunting, but no doubt my engineering background paid off as in a few short months I developed the skills to expand to working for other people. I now work remotely for a start up based out of Chicago called Blocklight.io.

In order to continue expanding my skills, I am looking for additional fun side development projects. I will happily work on any project, but my personal interests are in Health/Fitness, Gaming, and Tech especially Blockchain, Ecommerce, AI, and Real Estate, so if your project falls into any of those categories I would be extremely interested.

I can create completely custom web applications, simple business sites or ecommerce stores, and everything in between. To see a couple samples, check out my portfolio. If you wanna chat, go ahead and send me a messege at dehaandevelopment@gmail.com.
